What are full and limited title guarantees?
Full title guarantee: the transferors guarantee, to the best of their knowledge, that there are no financial charges/encumbrances (such as mortgages) or other third party interests (such as rights of way/leases) which affect the property other than those already revealed to the transferees.

Limited title guarantee: a more restricted set of promises by the transferors. This guarantees that the transferors have not themselves created, or allowed to be created, any charge, encumbrance or third party interest that still exists at the date of the transfer.
