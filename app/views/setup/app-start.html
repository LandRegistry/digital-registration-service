Digital Registration Service

You can use this service to submit an AP1 form to update an existing title.

This service can be used for applications of up to 25 titles where the same proprietors are registered against each title.

Coronavirus (COVID-19): temporary rules on identity

HM Land Registry has introduced a range of temporary measures for verifying identity during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

The measures are set out in Practice Guide 67A (opens in new window), which accompanies Practice Guide 67 (opens in new window).

Before you start

You'll need to provide:

  • title numbers
  • applicant details
  • scanned documents, if needed

You cannot use this service to:

  • register a transfer of charge
  • register a transfer by operation of law
  • register a transfer of residue
  • register a transfer of part applications
  • submit applications for personal representation
  • submit applications against a pending new title where it involves a transfer of charge
  • submit applications with more than 25 title numbers
  • merge multiple title numbers

For these applications, please use the Document Registration Service.
