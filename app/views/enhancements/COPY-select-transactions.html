About disclosable overriding interests
Select the box if this application relates to a registrable disposition and disclosable overriding interests affect the registered estate.

If there’s a disclosable overriding interest, you must:

  • attach a completed Form DI (opens in new tab)
  • provide evidence of the overriding interest

    Evidence of overriding interest could be a certified copy of the deed or document that created it. If valid, the overriding interest will be noted on the title.
