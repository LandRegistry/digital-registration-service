Is the restriction standard or non-standard?

Which restriction type do I need?
A standard restriction will use the wording set out in Practice guide 19 (opens in new tab) and usually refer to a specific clause in a deed. There are 44 standard restrictions. Using a standard restriction is less likely to result in requisitions. Check the practice guide to see if there is a restriction that meets the needs of your application. You should use non-standard restrictions where:
  • the restriction refers to compliance with the whole deed, rather than a specific clause
  • the restriction requires additional descriptive text that cannot be entered using the standard form
  • the wording set out in the practice guide does not meet the needs of the restriction
